Expleris' Logo

Expleris’ logo carries both a Nordic and a universal meaning. The Tree of Life, or “Yggdrasil”, in Norse mythology symbolizes the connection between worlds. Similarly, we aim to forge a lasting network between our partners. Throughout centuries and in different cultures, trees have also represented stability, nourishment, and enlightenment.
