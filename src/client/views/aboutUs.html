Expleris' Logo

Expleris’ logo communicates both a Nordic and a universal meaning. Through centuries and in different cultures, trees have represented strength, stability, shelter, nourishment, good fortunes and enlightenment. In Norse Mythology, from the Nordic countries, it is called ”Yggdrasil”, - the tree of life whose wisdom and knowledge are guarded by the god Odin.
